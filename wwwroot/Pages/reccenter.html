Recreation Center

About this facility:

The Student Recreation Center helps provide students wellness, building community, pushing confidence and launching futures. With Rocket-Ex group exercise classes, MX4 classes, Personal Training and Fitness assessments - the center helps students meet recreational, fitness and learning goals.

The Recreation Center:

  • Gives students a facility for wellness based activities through: basketball courts, soccer courts, yoga classes, pool lessons, etc.
  • Provides job opportunities for students searching for on-campus experience.
  • Offers personal training for all UTREC members and UT students.
  • Offers circuit training classes that revolve around: cardio, power, strength and endurance.
  • Offers American Red Cross CPR certification classes.
  • Offers a UTREC Kids Camp program in the summer.
